Each of my homemade items is a labor of love, infused with my passion that started at a very young age, learning at my mother's side even as challenging as it was with me being left handed. We sat across each other instead of next to each other so I could mirror her technique. I love how you design or take other's ideas and creatd unique and beautiful pieces. From cozy, vibrant afghans to practical and durable dishcloths, my items are all crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail. What sets my work apart is my dedication to creating one-of-a-kind designs that can't be found anywhere else. I'm always excited to see what new patterns and ideas my customers bring to me, and I love putting my own twist on classic designs to make them even more unique and perfect. Whether you're looking for a cozy addition to your home or a thoughtful gift for someone special, I know you'll find something, or we can create something you love in my treasures of handmade treasures.
